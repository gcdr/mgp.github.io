Hi, I'm Michael Parker.

Currently I work at Khan Academy, which provides a free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere. I've helped develop both their iOS app and their Android app, both of which received some nice press.

Previously, I was the lead developer on Burner at Ad Hoc Labs. There I developed the Android app, and productionised the backend while increasing its test coverage from 0 to over 90%. Before that, I co-founded ReadyUp!, worked for almost five years at Google on enterprise search and Google Docs, and earned a BS and MS in computer science from UCLA.

I'm also a founding volunteer of Project:Possibility, through which I've mentored hundreds of college students writing software to improve the lives of the physically disabled.

My outdated resume is available in HTML or PDF format.
