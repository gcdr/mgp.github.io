Hi, I'm Michael Parker.

Currently I work at Khan Academy, which seeks to provide a free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere. Previously I was the lead developer on Burner at Ad Hoc Labs. Before that, I co-founded ReadyUp!, worked for almost five years at Google on Google Docs and enterprise search, and earned a BS and MS in computer science from UCLA.

I'm also a founding volunteer of Project:Possibility, through which I've mentored hundreds of college students writing software to improve the lives of the physically disabled.

My outdated resume is available in HTML or PDF format.
