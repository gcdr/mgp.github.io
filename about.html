Hi, I'm Michael Parker.

These days I'm writing Code is Craft, a book to bridge the gap between writing code in school and writing code in industry.

Previously, I was the lead developer on Burner at Ad Hoc Labs, where I developed the Android application and productionized the backend. Before that, I co-founded ReadyUp!, worked for almost five years at Google on enterprise search and Google Docs, and earned a BS and MS in computer science from UCLA.

My slightly-outdated resume is available in HTML or PDF format.
